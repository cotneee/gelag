About us

Our story began in 1878, when Nature was simply a humble tea business offering a selection of high-quality teas. Over the years, through dedication and a commitment to quality, we have earned the trust and loyalty of our customers. Today, we are proud to celebrate our 120th anniversary—a milestone that reflects the enduring relationship we’ve built with those who appreciate our products. It is with great pride that we continue to offer the same passion and excellence that has made us a beloved brand for over a century
